Steve Leigh is a software developer living in Brisbane, Australia.

In his day job, he works as a technical lead at Zap, where he writes BI software, imparts knowledge about new technologies, clean code and design patterns and provides guidance and mentoring on software design, automation and CI/CD.

At night, Steve plays PC games and does development on some personal projects, where he tries to push the bounds of what he knows, and what he thinks he knows.

Steve is married to Paula, and is the father of 2 girls; Sophie and Rachel.
