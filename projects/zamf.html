Zombies are Attacking My Fridge is a game that is inspired in part by the plethora of tower defense games made out there. A 3D real time tower defense game!

The idea is simple - there are zombies. They want to gain access to your fridge, and it's up to you to pull together household equipment to stop them. Swivel-chair mounted nailguns, doormats with spikes and deoderant-powered flame throwers all make up part of your arsenal as you defend the fridge against the vicious onslaught of Zombies.

Technologies Used

  • Ogre - Graphics rendering, supports OpenGL and DirectX
  • CEGUI - GUI Widgets, integrated well with Ogre
  • OIS - Keyboard/Mouse/Joystick Input
  • Bullet - Physics Engine
  • OpenAL - Sound

Features

Current

  • Weapon: Nailgun - aims at closest zombie in range and fires
  • Weapon: Magnet/Ball combo - a heavy ball is attracted to a super powerful magnet, destroying anything in its way.
  • Enemy: Zombie - heads straight for the player and attacks
  • Gameplay: HP, Money, Kill counter
  • Config file for tweaking stats
  • Real time shadows
  • Real time physics simulation
  • 3D positional sound and music
  • Level of Detail
